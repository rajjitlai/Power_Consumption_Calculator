⚡ Power (Watts)

Check the physical label or sticker on the back/bottom of your appliance. Look for "W" or "Watts". For PCs, you can use software like HWMonitor or check your PSU's rating.

💰 Cost per Unit

Look at your last electricity bill. It usually lists the rate per "kWh" (Kilowatt-hour). Common rates vary by region (e.g., $0.12 - $0.30).

🕒 Usage Time

Be realistic about active usage. A PC might be "on" for 10 hours but only consuming peak power during gaming or heavy rendering.
